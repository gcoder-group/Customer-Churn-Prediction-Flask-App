This project demonstrates a Customer Churn Prediction system built using Machine Learning. The goal is to predict whether a telecom customer is likely to stop using the service (churn) based on various attributes such as contract type, monthly charges, internet services, tenure, and payment methods.

The model is trained on the Telco Customer Churn Dataset and helps identify customers who are at high risk of leaving the service. Businesses can use such predictions to take preventive actions like offering discounts or improving customer support.

On this page, you can:

  • Load sample customer data
  • Enter customer attributes manually
  • Predict whether the customer will churn
  • View churn probability generated by the model
